Welcome to The Barrens

Once a prosperous colony, this system has now been taken over by space raiders. The debris belt around it ensures it remains a haven for the scourge of the galaxy, where no one dares to enter. Do you dare face the dangers of the Barren Nebula?

Exciting Space Action

Barren Nebula features small, action packed episodes with engaging gameplay. Each episode will feature a different environment and gameplay, and will be compact enough for a quick playthrough, but so enganging that will make you come back for more.

Many dangers to overcome

The infamous debris belt that surrounds The Barrens is filled with dangerous outlaws and many other hazards. The only way out is through it, and you have to blast your way through with nothing but your armor and weapons. Thankfully, the pirates are not very bright, and carry more supplies than they can use, so make use of it!

Survival is improbable, but not impossible!

At your disposal you have your trusty ray gun, a rocket launcher, and a powerful laser module. Salvage supplies from your enemies to find ammo, first-aid space-kits and shield modules. You need to keep pushing, and collect keycards from your enemies so you can access the mysterious ship.
